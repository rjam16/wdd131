An Untapped Well of Ideas

We all know an "idea man".

Someone everyone goes to when in a creative block. Think of this process as your idea man, a guide to find your muse.

Dedicating an entire website to such a simple process may seem juvenile, but ideas come from strange places.

I, myself, had to break the dam that was blocking my inspiration to start creating.

Whether you believe it silly or not, try giving it a go to see if it's what your brainstorming session needed.

Pick a Point of Interest

Find something that sticks out to you

Ask Any and All Questions

Challenge your brain to think outside the box

Repeat...

Keep using the first two steps to fully flesh-out the idea
